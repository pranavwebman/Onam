The Legend of Onam

Onam is celebrated in honor of King Mahabali, a beloved ruler who it is believed visits his people during this festival. According to legend, Kerala witnessed its golden era during the reign of King Mahabali.

Pookalam Art

Pookalam is an intricate and colorful arrangement of flowers laid on the floor. Traditional designs are made in circles, representing the cyclical nature of life. The size of a Pookalam grows day by day during the Onam festival.

Onam Sadya

The Onam Sadya is a grand vegetarian feast served on banana leaves. It consists of rice along with 20-25 different dishes, including sambar, avial, thoran, and the famous payasam dessert. The meal is typically followed by a banana and a betel leaf.
